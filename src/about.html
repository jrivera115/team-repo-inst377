SAFETY ZONE

ABOUT

Information Problem

Many individuals are unaware of the potential health concerns that exist in regards to food served at various foodservice facilities. Because of this, there have been cases of mass food poisoning for multiple food service facilities. By providing a method that will inform people of facility inspection results, people will be able to make easier decisions in regards to what they would want to eat.

For our team, each one of us had visions and/or goals that we wanted to be carried out with this project. Many of us are food-enthusiasts and were interested in looking at food inspection results data since it is relevant to us when it comes down to what restaurants we want to pick when choosing where we want to eat. Our initial thought that we had to target this problem was providing an easier method that would show us relevant restaurant data all in one place. Food applications such as Yelp only provide general information about restaurants such as its name, location, 5-star rating, and reviews. Our group wanted to target our information problem and take it one-step further by adding facility inspection results. In addition, we wanted to specialize our application by our own food interests which can be seen through the filter options that will categorize the food restaurants based on food type.

Solution

In hopes to inform these individuals, we will be solving our information problem by creating an application, titled Safety Zone. Safety Zone, will bring about food safety awareness by generating food places that comply or fail to comply within a certain mile radius of the user's location. Our application will help users identify food service facilities nearby, as well as basic information about these facilities such as prices, type of food, customer reviews, etc. Our results will display an updated record of their food inspection results, allowing users to know whether the food service facility they have chosen has been found to be in violation, and if so, if actions must be taken in order to address it. Our application will help users make an informed choice about which food service facilities to go to. Our application will target the general public and those who are most affected by food service facilities that are found to be in violation. We hope to inform our audience of food safety awareness with Safety Zone. The application will begin by asking the user to input a Zip Code or City address. The input will be used to retrieve a colelction of sorted data that will reveal food services that are tagged with varying levels of food inspection compliance results. Then, these user will be able to filter the inspection results through various categories.
