SAFETY ZONE

DOCUMENTATION

Welcome to Safety Zone, A Place to Eat Clean! The steps to use our application are easy! Beginning at our Index page, the user will be presented with a search bar that will allow the user to switch between inputting a City or a Zip Code within PG County for a particular restaurant he or she may be looking for in relation to its food inspection results. After typing in their input, the user will click the search button (i.e. the magnifying glass) on their keyboard to be taken to an output of their results.

At the results page, the user will see a map of PG County on their right and location indicators of where corresponding restaurants are in relation to the City or Zip Code that was inputted. On the left, there will be a list of restaurant names that fall in the given City/Zip Code area. The list will include a particular Restaurant Name and the corresponding inspection results. Below the results, the date of the food inspection will also be listed.

Be sure to check out our About page for more information!
